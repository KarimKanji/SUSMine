About SUSMine


SUSMine, SustainableMine is a unique take on how we can make cryptocurrency and sustainability a recipe for a better future.


We as a company function as an investment firm with our goals being a sustainable yet technological future. Our company's main revenue stream is our cryptocurrency mining facility. We use the profit from our mining operation to invest in both national and international projects that show great promise for a sustainable future. The reason this company started with crypto-mining as a foundation is that most "green" investment opportunities rarely yield great profit. By calculating our investments and having a solid fallback we are able to truly make sustainable investments instead of making purely economical investments. This way we have found that our eyes never wonder away from what really matters, a sustainable future and a world that coming generations deserve.


Our Values and goals


MISSION: Our mission is to make a self-sustaining company that is able to give a reaching hand to promising projects that strive for a sustainable future


VISION: We visionize a company that is truly able to make a difference without allowing corporate greed to corrupt and blur our vision of a brighter future. We also see our selves being extremely transparent with our community and especially with our clients
